About Me

I am Muhammad Arif (Rana Arif), a BS Cyber Security student at Emerson University Multan. I am passionate about exploring new technologies, especially in ethical hacking, penetration testing, and secure software development. My curiosity and dedication drive me to find practical solutions to real-world IT challenges.

I regularly take online courses, workshops, and hackathons to enhance my knowledge. With hands-on practice using tools like Nmap, Metasploit, and iTextPDF, I enjoy building solutions that connect cybersecurity and software development.

Ultimately, I aim to become a leading cybersecurity professional, mentor others, and launch projects combining security, software, and entrepreneurship.
