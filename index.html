About Me

I am Muhammad Arif (Rana Arif), a BS Cyber Security student at Emerson University Multan. I am passionate about exploring new technologies, especially in ethical hacking, penetration testing, and network security. My expertise spans security analysis, vulnerability assessment, and secure system design.

Ultimately, I aim to become a leading cybersecurity professional, mentor others, and launch projects combining security, software, and entrepreneurship.
