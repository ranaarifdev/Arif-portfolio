About Me

I am Muhammad Arif (Rana Arif), a BS Cyber Security student at Emerson University Multan. I am passionate about exploring new technologies, especially in ethical hacking, penetration testing, and network security research.

Alongside my studies, I build Android applications using Kotlin and Jetpack Compose, experiment with networking tools, and learn frameworks such as Google APIs and modern security libraries.

I regularly take online courses, workshops, and participate in hackathons to enhance my knowledge and practical skills. With hands-on practice using penetration testing frameworks and network simulation tools, I continuously develop expertise in cybersecurity.

Ultimately, I aim to become a leading cybersecurity professional, mentor others in security practices, and launch innovative projects combining security, software engineering, and entrepreneurship.
